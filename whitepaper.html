VAMP Network

Business & Gaming Utility Token on Solana • Whitepaper v1.0

Abstract

VAMP Network ($VAMP) is a reliable and sustainable utility token built on the Solana ecosystem. It is designed for integrations across business and gaming platforms—enabling payments, premium access, and loyalty/rewards systems—while remaining simple, transparent, and partner-friendly.

Mission

To create a reliable and sustainable token on Solana, designed to integrate with business projects, gaming platforms, and high-value partnerships.

Core Utility

  • Integration token for business & gaming platforms (payments, premium access, points/rewards systems).
  • Progressive Staking Rewards (4–8%, depending on lock duration), to be deployed once infrastructure is in place.
  • Strategic partnerships planned to expand use cases (platforms, events, marketplaces).

Key Features

  • Fixed Supply: 100,000,000 VAMP (balanced scarcity and accessibility).
  • Solana: High throughput, low fees, strong tooling.
  • Open Ecosystem: Easy integration via API and smart-contract extensions.
  • Transparency & Sustainability: Progressive development with public milestone updates.

Roadmap (High Level)

  • Listings & metadata: Solscan, DexScreener/Birdeye sync, CoinGecko/CMC submissions.
  • Partner kit: API/SDK & brand guidelines for integrations.
  • Staking R&D: reward tiers (4–8%), security review/audit before launch.
  • Pilot integrations with a business or indie-game partner.
  • Transparency: periodic reports on TVL, LP lock status and delivery.
