🧛‍♂️ VAMP Network – FAQ

❓ What is VAMP Network?

VAMP Network is a decentralized token on Solana, themed around a kawaii vampire world. It focuses on staking, NFTs, and community governance.

❓ How many VAMP tokens exist?

There is a fixed supply of 100,000,000 VAMP, balancing accessibility and rarity.

❓ Is there a staking system?

Yes! A progressive staking system is coming soon, offering 4%–8% rewards based on 3 loyalty tiers.

❓ Will VAMP be available on Ethereum?

Yes, an Ethereum launch is part of our roadmap to bring more visibility and integration opportunities.

❓ Will there be NFTs?

Absolutely! NFT collections tied to our lore and staking mechanics are part of the project’s vision.

❓ How is liquidity handled?

Liquidity will grow gradually, with pool funding scaling as our popularity and community grow.

❓ Where can I buy/trade VAMP?

If listed on a DEX like Orca, you can swap SOL for VAMP. Otherwise, follow our site & Discord for official announcements.

❓ Is the project open to partnerships?

Yes! We're exploring collaborations for utility, NFTs, and community-driven use cases. Contact us via Discord!
